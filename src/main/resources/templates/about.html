About GhanaRide

Connecting Ghana through safe and reliable transport

Our Mission

At GhanaRide, our mission is to simplify intercity and campus transportation across Ghana. We believe that traveling from Accra to Kumasi, or from your hostel to campus, should be a seamless, safe, and affordable experience for everyone.

By connecting passengers with verified and trusted drivers, we are building a community based on trust, reliability, and convenience.

Why Choose Us?

  • Verified Drivers: Every driver and vehicle undergoes a strict verification process.
  • Affordable Fares: Transparent pricing with no hidden charges.
  • Punctuality: Say goodbye to long waits at the station. Book your seat in advance.
  • Flexible Payments: Pay online via Paystack or with cash at pickup.
