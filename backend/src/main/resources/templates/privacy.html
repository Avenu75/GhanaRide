Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2025

Your privacy matters to us. Here's how we handle your data.

1. Who We Are

GhanaRide is a transport booking platform operated in Ghana. We are committed to protecting your personal data in accordance with applicable Ghanaian data protection laws and international best practices.

2. Information We Collect

We collect the following types of information:

  • Account Information: Full name, email address, phone number, date of birth, and gender when you register.
  • Trip & Booking Data: Routes, departure times, seat selections, and booking history.
  • Payment Information: Transaction references and payment status. We do not store card numbers — all payment data is handled by Paystack.
  • Driver Documents: License details, roadworthy certificates, and vehicle information for drivers.
  • Location Data: With your permission, we may access your location to display your real-time position on trip tracking.
  • Device & Usage Data: Browser type, IP address, pages visited, and session duration for security and analytics purposes.

3. How We Use Your Information

  • To create and manage your account.
  • To process and confirm bookings.
  • To send booking receipts and trip updates via email.
  • To verify driver and vehicle credentials.
  • To provide real-time trip tracking.
  • To detect and prevent fraud and abuse.
  • To improve platform features and user experience.
  • To send promotional communications (only if you have opted in).

4. Legal Basis for Processing

  • Contract Performance: Processing necessary to fulfil your booking.
  • Legitimate Interests: Security, fraud prevention, and service improvement.
  • Legal Obligation: Compliance with Ghanaian regulations and tax requirements.
  • Consent: For marketing communications and optional location tracking.

5. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal data. We may share data with:

  • Drivers/Companies: Passenger name and phone for trip coordination.
  • Paystack: For payment processing (governed by Paystack's privacy policy).
  • Email Services: For sending booking confirmations and notifications.
  • Law Enforcement: When required by Ghanaian law or court order.

6. Data Retention

  • Account data is retained for as long as your account is active.
  • Booking records are kept for 7 years for regulatory compliance.
  • Marketing preferences and consent records are kept until withdrawn.
  • You may request deletion of your account at any time via the contact page.

7. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access: Request a copy of your personal data.
  • Correction: Update inaccurate information in your profile settings.
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your account and data.
  • Portability: Receive your data in a machine-readable format.
  • Objection: Opt out of marketing communications at any time.

8. Security

We implement industry-standard security measures including:

  • HTTPS encryption on all pages.
  • Bcrypt password hashing — we never store plain passwords.
  • Session timeout and CSRF protection.
  • Rate limiting on login endpoints.
  • Regular security audits and updates.

9. Cookies

We use the following cookies:

  • Session Cookie (JSESSIONID): Required for authentication — deleted on logout.
  • Remember Me Cookie (GHANARIDE_REMEMBER_ME): Optional — lasts 30 days if you choose "Remember Me" on login.
  • Theme Preference (localStorage): Stores your dark/light mode preference locally.
  • We do not use advertising or tracking cookies.

10. Children's Privacy

GhanaRide is not intended for users under the age of 18. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

11. Changes to this Policy

We may update this policy periodically. Significant changes will be communicated via email or a notice on the platform.

12. Contact

For privacy-related requests or questions, contact our Data Protection Officer:

  • Email: privacy@ghanaride.me
  • Or use our Contact Page
