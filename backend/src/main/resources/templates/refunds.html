Refund Policy

Last updated: January 2025

We strive to make every trip smooth. Here's what happens when things don't go as planned.

1. Cancellations by Passengers

If you wish to cancel a booking, the following policy applies:

Time of Cancellation Refund Amount
More than 48 hours before departure 100% Full Refund
24–48 hours before departure 50% Refund
Less than 24 hours before departure No Refund
No-show (did not board the vehicle) No Refund

2. Cancellations by Drivers or Companies

If your trip is cancelled by the driver or transport company for any reason:

  • You will receive a 100% full refund automatically.
  • You will be notified immediately via email and your GhanaRide dashboard.
  • Refunds are initiated within 24 hours of the cancellation.
  • GhanaRide will assist in finding an alternative trip where possible.

3. Delays

  • If a trip is delayed by more than 3 hours and you choose not to travel, you are entitled to a full refund.
  • Partial delays do not qualify for a refund.
  • Contact our support team immediately to initiate a delay-related refund.

4. How to Request a Refund

  1. Log in to your GhanaRide account.
  2. Navigate to My Bookings.
  3. Select the booking you wish to cancel.
  4. Click "Cancel Booking" and confirm.
  5. If eligible, your refund will be processed automatically.
  6. For issues, contact us at support@ghanaride.me.

5. Refund Processing Time

  • Approved refunds are processed within 5–10 business days.
  • Refunds are returned to the original payment method (Mobile Money, Card, etc.).
  • Processing times may vary depending on your bank or mobile money provider.
  • GhanaRide will send you an email confirmation when your refund is processed.

6. Non-Refundable Situations

  • Booking fees and processing charges (if applicable) are non-refundable.
  • Trips cancelled within 24 hours of departure.
  • No-shows without prior notice.
  • Violations of our Terms of Service that result in trip termination.

7. Disputes

If you believe you are entitled to a refund that was not issued, please contact our support team with:

  • Your booking reference number.
  • Date and route of the trip.
  • Reason for the refund request.
  • Any supporting evidence (screenshots, communications, etc.).

We aim to resolve all disputes within 7 business days.
